about juice laboratory


at juice laboratory, our mission is to give everyone the chance to enjoy delicious, healthy drinks made from natural ingredients.

juice laboratory started in 1995 in a small city in manchester; where ingredients were sourced locally and juices were made with no added sugar or preservatives. people loved the idea of enjoying something natural and delicious and that is the reason we are here now - because of you guys.
